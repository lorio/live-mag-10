10

Ron Kolm

Bad Timing

We turn off the lights 
In my friend’s apartment 
And I watch you fiddling 
In the darkness with your laptop 
Bathed in a greenish glow 
Like a bombardier looking 
For a target below. I know 
  Your mind in somewhere else--
 That became clear enough 
When you gently brushed 
Away my hand as I tried 
To touch your breast. 
“This is not a good 
Time,” you said, sliding 
Off the sofa, returning 
To your work.
